About ATLANTIS

ATLANTIS Scoreboard was created by Jonathan Geisler as a fully custom-built Blue Team competition platform designed for live security competitions and CTF-style events.

Born out of a passion for cybersecurity and visual excellence, ATLANTIS merges practical scoring with a sleek, futuristic interface that reflects the intensity and innovation of cyber defense teams.

The platform supports real-time service status checks, uptime monitoring, and inject management all with blazing-fast performance and intuitive layout, making it perfect for both competitors and judges.

Inspired by platforms like the DWAYNE-INATOR-5000, ATLANTIS is built using modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and JSON.
