About ATLANTIS

ATLANTIS Scoreboard was developed by Jonathan Geisler as a fully customized Blue Team competition platform. It supports live service checks, uptime reporting, and inject grading.

This scoreboard is inspired by tools like the DWAYNE-INATOR-5000 and built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON.
